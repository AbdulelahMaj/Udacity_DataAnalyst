What is WeRateDogs Project's about?¶

WeRateDogs is a platform where people can rate thier dogs. they have on twitter over 8M followers. So we are trying in this project to find how to understand the users they participate in the account for example: What is the the most frequent dog's name, or what is the source that users rate dogs such as iphone...etc

I downloaded the Twitter data for the WeRateDogs website. The final dataset contained 1899 tweets.
